What is Fast$ell?

Fast$ell is a marketplace for anyone who has a product they want to sell. We offer the marketplace for small businesses, individual sellers and of course for anyone who wants to buy something. We do not have auctions because as the name says we stand for fast sales.

How much does Fast$ell charge per sale?

Unlike other marketplaces, we charge a 5% fee per product sold. This percentage is deducted from the respective purchase price. The same applies on all products, it is always 5% no matter what cathegory.

Is Fast$ell meant only for certain products?

No, Fast$ell is suitable for anyone who has a product to sell. It is a free marketplace for many different products.
